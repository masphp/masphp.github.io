About Me

I am currently an assistant professor at the faculty of Engineering, University of Maragheh, Maragheh, Iran. I am also acting as the IT manager and network admin of the University of Maragheh.

I have a Ph.D. in Information Technology (Computer Networks) from Urmia University, Urmia, Iran.

Research Interests

  • Computer Networks
  • Nanonetworks
  • Game Theory
  • Network economics and pricing
  • Internet of things (IoT)
  • Optical networks

Education
